Our Story

How we started and where we're headed

Founded with a Purpose

Job Finder was founded in 2020 by a team of HR professionals and tech enthusiasts who recognized a fundamental problem in the job market: the disconnect between qualified candidates and the right opportunities.

Our founders experienced firsthand how traditional job boards and recruitment methods often led to mismatches, wasted time, and frustration for both job seekers and employers.

With a vision to transform this experience, we built Job Finder as an AI-powered platform that intelligently matches candidates with opportunities based on skills, experience, and preferences - not just keywords.
